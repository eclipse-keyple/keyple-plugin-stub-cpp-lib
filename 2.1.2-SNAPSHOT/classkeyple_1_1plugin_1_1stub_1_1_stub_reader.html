Detailed Description

The Stub Reader supports programmatically the insertion and removal of StubSmartCard
To invoke those methods use Reader::getExtension(StubReader.class) from the Reader class.

virtual void keyple::plugin::stub::StubReader::insertCard ( std::shared_ptr< StubSmartCard >  smartCard)

Insert a stub card into the reader. The card is taken into account by the reader only if its protocol has been activated previously by the method keyple::core::plugin::spi::reader::ConfigurableReaderSpi::activateProtocol(). In such case it raises a CARD_INSERTED event.
